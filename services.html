Our Services

We believe employing a good Interior Designer is an investment on your home. Modernarta Interiors focuses on conceptualizing and bringing to life the clients style and vision at each step of the process. By elegantly merging your vision with our design expertise, we’ll be able encapsulate our clients dreams. We place importance on the practicality and comfort of our designs as good design has the ability to impact the way our clients feel and live in their homes. The success of the project not only lies in the final result but also the process and we pride ourselves with our level of customer service that ensures our clients feel like the only project that matters

The Brief

Key to the success of any project is ensuring that the interiors are an authentic reflection of our client’s requirements and aspirations. During the brief stage we take the time to learn about how the client... wishes to use the property, their personal style, lifestyle and aspirations. We will build a picture of the project through a site visit or floor plans and photographs as well as ensure that we understand all factors such as time scale, budget, and any structural or administrative requirements. Following this thorough briefing stage we will send a proposal to the client outlining the brief as we understand it, with an estimated budget for the FF&E and our associated design fee.

Conceptualization

Intepreting the detailed brief, we will present the concept designs which consist of floor plans, illustrating the proposed furniture plans, any structural changes that may be required and renders or moodboard presentations containing samples of all proposed finishes as well as imagery of suggested joinery and furniture. Itemised costing is presented and a project timeline is conveyed. To ensure that we are on the same page, we invite our clients to give as much feedback as possible as this is a very collaborative stage.

Design

Once the client has approved the design proposal, itemised costing and design fee and a deposit has been paid, we will proceed to manufacturing, source materials, furnishes and decorative items.

Detail

Having approved all of the concept designs we progress to the design development and detail design stage. All final bespoke furniture, joinery and interior architecture drawings will be presented, along with a all final finishes and... curated art and accessories for clients to approve. At this stage we will also present a fully itemised breakdown of costs in line with the agreed budget.

Design

This stage consists of installation and completion of the project. The design team and project coordinators will be on site regularly to ensure our designs are being carried out to our expectations and the project is progressing as planned to meet timelines. We will keep our clients in the loop regularly to ensure peace of mind.

Realization

The schedule of works will be outlined and agreed. With all onsite requirements met, works will commence and our project coordinators and designers will attend regular site meetings to ensure our designs are being carried out... to their exact specifications and that the project is progressing as planned. We will keep the client updated throughout as well as oversee the snagging during the snagging period, allowing our client to enjoy their property with peace of mind.

Handover

Our favourite stage. Nothing warms our heart like bringing the clients in for the first time and seeing their faces light up! During this stage, we will walk the client through their newly designed space, show them the ropes and leave them to enjoy their new space.
