Modernarta Interiors is a Bespoke Furniture Manufacturing and Interior Design Firm based in the City of Gold, Johannesburg South Africa.

Modernarta Specializes in Luxury residential and commercial interiors for both private clients and developers in South Africa and Globally. Timeless elegance is our goal on each project.

Design

Masonwabe Mkoka is the founder and Lead Designer at Modernarta Interiors. Our design approach not only targets Luxuriously curated spaces but it is also a holistic one as she encompasses Feng Shui principles into her designs. We believe interiors need to work for us and change our lives for the better!


Having established Modernarta Interiors in 2016, Masonwabe has gone on to expand her portfolio from not only Furniture Manufacturing but to Interior Design and Feng Shui. She has been providing outstanding custom made furniture and interior design services in Johannesburg and across South Africa
